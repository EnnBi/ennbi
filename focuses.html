    Company Service Focuses:

  • We think in today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, businesses require robust IT services to stay competitive and secure. This document provides an overview of the key service offerings provided by our company.


    • Key Focus Areas of this our services include:



  • Cloud Scalability Service Focus:

    CubicOps believes as cloud computing gains prominence, the ability to scale resources dynamically has become critical for businesses of all sizes. Our Cloud Scalability Service Focus helps organizations leverage cloud platforms to ensure optimized resource allocation, seamless scalability, and enhanced performance. Our experts work closely with clients to design and implement cloud architectures that align with their business goals and cater to their specific resource demands.
  • Key Features:
    • - Comprehensive assessment of existing infrastructure
    • - Identification of cloud scalability requirements
    • - Design and implementation of scalable cloud architectures
    • - Continuous monitoring and optimization of cloud resources
    • - Proactive capacity planning and resource management


  • Cyber Security VIS CIS-RAM Service Focus:

    As with everything else we take cyber threats on the rise very seriously, organizations must prioritize cybersecurity to protect sensitive data and maintain a robust security posture. Our Cyber Security VIS CIS-RAM Service Focus assists clients in mitigating risks, securing networks, and ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations. Our cybersecurity professionals employ comprehensive methodologies to identify vulnerabilities, assess risks, and develop effective security strategies.
  • Key Features:
    • - Vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
    • - Risk management and mitigation strategies
    • - Security compliance and regulation assessments
    • - Incident response planning and execution
    • - Security awareness training and education


  • Cloud Assessment Service Focus:

    CubicOps gives extra attention before migrating to the cloud, it is essential to evaluate the feasibility and suitability of cloud adoption for an organization. Our Cloud Assessment Service Focus offers a comprehensive analysis of existing IT infrastructure, applications, and workflows to determine the potential benefits and challenges associated with cloud migration. We provide detailed reports and recommendations, enabling clients to make informed decisions about their cloud strategy.
  • Key Features:
    • - Assessment of current IT infrastructure and applications
    • - Identification of potential cloud migration opportunities
    • - Analysis of cost implications and return on investment
    • - Evaluation of security and compliance requirements
    • - Development of a customized cloud adoption roadmap


  • NIST/NISC Compliance Focus Service:

    CubicOps understands that many organizations operating in regulated industries or dealing with sensitive data need to comply with security standards set forth by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and National center of Incident readiness and Strategy for Cybersecurity (NISC). Our NIST/NISC Compliance Focus Service assists clients in achieving and maintaining compliance with NIST/NISC guidelines. Our experts perform comprehensive assessments and audits, develop customized security frameworks, and offer ongoing support to ensure adherence to NIST/NISC requirements.


  • Key Features:
    • - NIST compliance assessments and gap analysis
    • - Development and implementation of NIST security controls
    • - Ongoing monitoring and reporting of compliance status
    • - Incident response planning and compliance support
    • - Training and awareness programs on NIST compliance
